Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Taste

At Desi Mithas, we believe that every bite of Gulab Jamun should transport you back to cherished memories of home, family gatherings, and celebrations filled with love. Our journey began with a simple mission: to preserve the authentic taste of traditional Pakistani Gulab Jamun while bringing it to modern homes across the country.

Each Gulab Jamun is handcrafted using a time-honored recipe passed down through generations. We use only the finest ingredients, ensuring that every piece is soft, juicy, and bursting with the rich, sweet flavor that makes this dessert so beloved. Our commitment to freshness means that every batch is prepared daily, guaranteeing you receive the highest quality product.

We take pride in maintaining the purity and authenticity of this classic Pakistani dessert. From the careful preparation of the dough to the perfect golden-brown frying and the aromatic sugar syrup infusion, every step is executed with precision and love. This is not just a dessert; it's a celebration of our heritage, crafted with passion and served with pride.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Gulab Jamuns

Are your Gulab Jamuns made fresh daily? +

Yes, every batch is prepared fresh to ensure maximum taste and quality. We never use preservatives or sell day-old products.

Do you offer delivery? +

Yes, we provide quick and reliable delivery services. Your order will be carefully packaged and delivered fresh to your doorstep.

What ingredients do you use? +

We use only premium quality ingredients including milk solids, pure ghee, refined flour, cardamom, rose water, and sugar. No artificial flavors or preservatives are added.

How should Gulab Jamuns be stored? +

Store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They stay fresh for up to 5-7 days. For best taste, warm them slightly before serving.

Do you offer bulk orders? +

Yes, we accept bulk orders for weddings, corporate events, and large gatherings. Please contact us in advance to discuss your requirements.

Are your Gulab Jamuns suitable for family events? +

Absolutely! Our Gulab Jamuns are perfect for all celebrations including weddings, birthdays, Eid, and family gatherings. They're loved by people of all ages.

What makes Desi Mithas special? +

Our commitment to authenticity, quality, and freshness sets us apart. We use traditional recipes, premium ingredients, and handcraft each piece with care to deliver the authentic taste of Pakistani Gulab Jamun.
